Music History

John-Dag began playing the guitar at 6 years old for his church band. By age 8, he was going on world tours and had already established himself as a proficient guitarist and keyboardist. At the age of 16 he and his friend Jude Sackey started a band named CrossFire out of their mutual love for music and composing. John-Dag played all the guitars for the album and wrote several of the songs such as Sacrifice, Love Came Down. You can find CrossFire's latest album on Apple Music here.

Heart to Heart

CrossFire released this album with a more mature sound. The growth of the band both musically and emotionally is evident in this beautiful album. John-Dag played all guitars and many of the vocals

Dear God...

This was the first release by CrossFire and John-Dag's debut as a singer-songwriter and guitarist
